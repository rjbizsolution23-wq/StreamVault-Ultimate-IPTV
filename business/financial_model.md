## Financial Risk Analysis

### Revenue Risks & Mitigation

**1. Competition Risk**
- **Impact**: 15-25% revenue reduction
- **Probability**: Medium
- **Mitigation**: Exclusive content, superior technology, competitive pricing

**2. Content Cost Inflation**  
- **Impact**: 5-15% margin compression
- **Probability**: High
- **Mitigation**: Long-term contracts, diverse content sources, original productions

**3. Churn Rate Increase**
- **Impact**: 10-30% revenue impact
- **Probability**: Medium  
- **Mitigation**: Engagement analytics, personalization, customer success programs

### Cost Risks & Management

**1. Technology Infrastructure Scaling**
- **Risk**: 20-40% cost overruns
- **Mitigation**: Multi-cloud strategy, auto-scaling, performance monitoring

**2. Regulatory Compliance Costs**
- **Risk**: $2-5M additional annual costs
- **Mitigation**: Proactive compliance, legal partnerships, regulatory monitoring

**3. Currency & International Expansion**
- **Risk**: 5-10% revenue volatility  
- **Mitigation**: Currency hedging, local partnerships, regional pricing
